About

Strata Titles Act (Amendment) 2013 (Act A1450) was gazette on 7 February 2013 and started to enforced on 1st June 2015 throughout Peninsular Malaysia. This amendment is to facilitate the application to get strata titles and vacant possession (VP) simultaneously. In this new amendment, the Formula Units Share (SiFUS) is the important things needed before any parcel sold or application Certificate Proposed Strata Plan (CPSP) submitted to the Department of Survey and Mapping Malaysia (JUPEM) for approval. In addition, all parties involved need to understand the steps to be taken if any changes or amendments to the building plan and the issues  that cause changes of SiFUS. This study focuses on actions that should be taken if there is a change on the building plan in the strata scheme. These actions should be done together with the Licensed Land Surveyors, architects, developers, Land and Mines Office (PTG), and the Department of survey and Mapping Malaysia (JUPEM).

Land and Mines Office (PTG)

Land and Mines Office (PTG) in PTG JOHOR also propose new action for any changes of building plan and the action call called "FAST TRACK". This Fast Track action was done for certain cases. One of the cases was the exchange of long names on the title, change of country, district or state, and increasing or eliminate unnecessary conditions on the building plan.
